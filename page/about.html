About Us

Who We Are

GAS KCO LLP is one of the largest Kazakhstan oil companies, being an internationally recognized vertically integrated holding. The industrial complex of the Company includes steadily developing enterprises of oil and gas production, petroleum refining, petrochemicals production, the tire manufacturing complex, network of filling stations and services. GAS KCO LLP also has a stake in the financial (banking and insurance) sector companies.

Our dedication to global standards and transparency is not merely a policy, but a foundational principle that guides every aspect of our operations. We recognize our role in the international energy sector and understand the importance of fostering trust and accountability.

Our starting point commenced many years ago especially with the commercial activities of GAS KCO LLP , the company whose shareholders are the major oil producing and oil refining enterprises of the Kazakhstan. At present GAS KCO LLP is one of the leading exporter of Rusian oil products. GAS KCO LLP also continued to expand its resource base due to our Top notch services and integrity, we are constantly spreading around the world.

Our commitment to global standards is demonstrated through our adherence to rigorous international certifications, best practices, and industry benchmarks. We strive for excellence in all areas, from the quality of our products and services to our environmental stewardship and safety protocols.
